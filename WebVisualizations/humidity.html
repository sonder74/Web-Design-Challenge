Humidity

Our analysis of the weather data shows a weak relationship between higher (more northern) latitudes and lower levels of humidity, though this conclusion could be based on outlying data. On the date in question, most measurements showed humidity levels between 60% and 100%, with a cluster of far-northern cities registering levels between 70% and 90%. It's worth noting that the only measurements showing 0% humidity were taken in some of the northernmost cities.
