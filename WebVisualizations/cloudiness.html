Cloudiness

Our analysis of the weather data showed no direct correlation between a city's latitude and its level of cloudiness on the date in question. Measurements of cloud cover were grouped around several nodes (90%, 75%, and 0%), but there was no discernable differentiation among latitudes within these groupings.
