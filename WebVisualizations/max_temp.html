Max Temperature

Our analysis showed a clear relationship between a city's latitude and the maximum temperature readings taken on January 5, 2017. At equatorial latitudes (those closest to zero), maximum temperatures tended to rise, approaching and in some cases exceeding 100 degrees Fahrenheit. Maximum temperatures dropped in latitudes running away from the equator, but did so much more sharply in the north, where it was winter. Since most of our readings were taken in that hemisphere, there is a skew in the sample toward colder temperatures.
