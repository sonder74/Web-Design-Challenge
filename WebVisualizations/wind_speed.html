Wind Speed

Our analysis of the weather data showed a very weak correlation between northerly latitudes and higher wind speeds, though this conclusion could be based on outlying data. On the date in question, most global wind speeds were between 2 and 10 miles per hour, with a small subset of faster winds (up to 36 miles per hour) identified in the northernmost cities.
